#diversifyoutdoorsuk #representationmatters

ESEA (pronounced "E-C") people are underrepresented in UK outdoors media and our voices and issues are unheard in conversations about diversity in the outdoors.

🏞️ At ESEA Outdoors UK we are:

  1. fighting racism by celebrating ESEA representation, inclusion and diversity in the outdoors
  2. building community and connecting people through the outdoors, and using social media to empower isolated ESEA people around the country (especially those outside London)

💛 Join our community if you…

  1. to shout about anti-racism, representation and inclusion
  2. want to hear about and help plan outdoors meetups and events
  3. identify as of ESEA heritage
